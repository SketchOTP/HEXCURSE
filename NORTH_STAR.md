# North Star — HexCurse (installer source)

This repo is the **cursor-governance** source, not a consumer app. **`--run-hexcurse`** here skips **HEXCURSE/** DIRECTIVES sync (no pack in this tree).

**Consumer repos** (e.g. after `setup.js` install) keep the north star at **`HEXCURSE/NORTH_STAR.md`** with **`HEXCURSE/ONE_PROMPT.md`** — see **`cursor-governance/README.md`**. The bridge still accepts a legacy repo-root **`NORTH_STAR.md`** for migration.

Template for new projects: **`cursor-governance/templates/NORTH_STAR.md`**.
